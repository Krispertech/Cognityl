Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cognityl collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or work with us on AI consulting and agent systems.

1. Who We Are

Cognityl provides AI consulting, agent systems, workflow automation, and data intelligence services for businesses. Our website is available at cognityl.com.

For privacy questions, contact us at kristaps@cognityl.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, company website, selected service interest, and the message you provide.

We may also collect business communication data, such as emails, meeting notes, project requirements, technical documentation, and information you voluntarily share with us during a consultation or project.

When you visit our website, basic technical data may be processed by our hosting and infrastructure providers, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and access times.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, understand your business needs, prepare proposals, deliver AI consulting and agent development services, improve our website, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Contact Forms

Our contact form is processed through Formspree. When you submit the form, your information is sent to Formspree and made available to Cognityl so we can respond to your request.

Please do not submit sensitive personal data, passwords, payment card numbers, government IDs, or confidential business secrets through the public contact form.

5. AI Consulting and Client Data

During consulting or agent development work, clients may provide business data, documents, workflows, system information, or technical requirements. We use this information only to evaluate, design, build, test, and support the requested services.

If a project requires the use of third-party AI providers, cloud platforms, APIs, or automation tools, we will use those services only as necessary for the project and in line with the agreed scope.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

If you are in the EU or EEA, we process personal data based on contract performance, legitimate interests, consent where required, and legal obligations.

7. Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers to operate our website and business, including Vercel for hosting, GitHub for code storage, Formspree for contact form processing, and email providers for business communication.

We do not sell personal information.

8. Cookies

Our website may use essential technologies required for basic functionality. If we later add analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking tools, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice or consent options where required.

9. Data Retention

We keep inquiry and communication data for as long as necessary to respond to you, manage business relationships, provide services, resolve disputes, and meet legal or accounting requirements.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information, including HTTPS, limited access, secure service providers, and careful handling of client information. However, no system is completely secure.

11. International Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside your country, including outside the EU or EEA. Where required, appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses may be used.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request a copy of your personal data.

To exercise your rights, contact kristaps@cognityl.com.

13. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shows when this page was last revised.

15. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact Cognityl at kristaps@cognityl.com.
